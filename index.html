Winter Season in Mysore

The winter season in Mysore, which lasts from October through February, is the best time to visit Mysore for many reasons. Travelers can attend Dusshera festivities, enjoy outdoor sightseeing trips without any exhaustion, and take wildlife safaris in national parks like Nagarhole and Bandipur in Karnataka. Mysore weather in December is perfect to embark on adventure activities such as trekking, camping, and skydiving. The average temperature hovers between 15 to 25 degrees. Travelers should also expect occasional showers in between, which only make the city more beautiful and vibrant. Winter is certainly the best season to visit Mysore for a honeymoon. September/October is when the Dusshera celebrations in Mysore bring thousands of visitors to the city.

Summer Season in Mysore

Summer in Mysore lasts from March through June. Mysore weather in May is hot in the afternoon hours. The average temperature in Mysore in summer ranges between 22 and 39 degree Celsius. So, the summer season is not scorching hot as many places in Karnataka. The days are sunny and the post-sunset hours are pleasant enough to carry on with all tourist activities in Mysore. April and May are usually the hottest months of the year. This is the best season to visit Mysore, especially for those looking to get good deals on hotels and flight bookings.

Monsoon Season in Mysore

Monsoon in Mysore arrives in July and lasts till September. The average rainfall in Mysore city is about 804 millimeters while the Mysore district witnesses 761 mm of rain annually. The weather condition in Mysore during the monsoon season is not ideal for sightseeing. It is recommended to avoid a holiday in Mysore. Those who do not mind getting wet and looking for cheaper hotel accommodation may find monsoon the best time to visit Mysore. Nights after a rainy day are usually cooler so carry a light sweater or jacket.
